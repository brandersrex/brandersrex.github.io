Thailand Agricultural Training Program

Program Overview

Our Thailand Agricultural Training Program offers Nigerian farmers a unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience and advanced knowledge from leading experts at Maejo University. Over three weeks, participants will learn modern agricultural practices, enhance their skills, and receive certification upon completion.

Curriculum:

Participants are required to choose from the following agricultural value chain curriculum: A to Z of Rice Production A to Z of Fish Farming A to Z of Poultry Farming Soil Preparation and Farm Irrigation System Vegetable, Mushrooms, and Fruit Training for Export Any other customized training demanded by Nigerian farmers.
Apllication: Interested farmers can apply by filling out the registration form on our website. Ensure you meet the requirements and choose your preferred training module."
